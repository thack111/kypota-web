Kentucky Parks on the Air

First annual KYPOTA will be this year on August 29 2020, and will operate from 1400 UTC to 2200 UTC (9 am -5 pm CDT). This will be our first annual contest for KYPOTA, but we hope to see it grow over the years to come. This event is sponsored by the Murray State - Amateur Radio Club, and they will be operating some special Bonus stations from some local parks.

Please see the Contest Rules for a list of valid parks, and other rules.

If you have any questions please contact us at KYPOTA@murray-ky.net
